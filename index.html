About

Hi, I'm Jashu. I'm now an undergraduate student majoring in Physics and minor in Data Science & Statistics at Fudan University in Shanghai, China.

I’m passionate about technology and entrepreneurship. I enjoy exploring the infinite space of technology and seeing how it transforms into a promising business model that can impact human society and tackle the thorny challenges we face today. My research interests mainly center around Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. I’ve done several projects about AI applications in disease prediction and social network mining. I have also gained valuable insights into business and strategy through my internship experiences in management consulting. I have received an offer to join Boston Consulting Group Shanghai office. I’m eager to explore what change I can bring to this world!

My life trajectory started in the suburbs of a small city, proceeded to the provincial capital Zhengzhou, and now I’m in Shanghai. Along the way, I realized how exposing myself to diverse cultures and communicating different ideas could enable the reimagination of a career and life. Therefore, I’m passionate about exploring different cultures and connecting people. Check out the fun stuff I did here.

Other than that, I am a public speaking enthusiast, a Ravenclaw, an ENTJ, a jogger, an amateur pianist, a professional Hulusi player, and more to be discovered.

Thank you for visiting my website and it's a pleasure introducing myself to you. If you find my stories interesting or inspiring, feel free to connect me and I look forward to another insightful conversation!
